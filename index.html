Hi and welcome to my fashion blog!

My name is Francheska Nuqui, I am 21, 5'0 inches tall and based in the Philippines. A way of expressing myself is by mix matching clothes and creating my own fashion style.

My style philosophy is having less is more but should be comfortable. I'm a fan of neutral colors, basic tees and sun glasses.

Styling, doing my own hair and make up and having DIY (do it yourself) photoshoots have always brought out the enthusiasm and confidence in me. It has always been my passion and I am looking forward to opportunities that will contribute in my career path.
